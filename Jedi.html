ENEMIES OF THE ORDER

As Kylo continues on his quest to complete what his grandfather started
it turns out not everyone is willing to let him get away with it.
Top of his list is the last Jedi, his uncle Luke Skywalker. There is also
that hairy buffoon Chewbacca. Three new rebels came onto the scene, a Resistance
fighter, a Stormtrooper and a girl who seems familiar.
Then of course there are his parents, his dear parents.
